Frequently Asked Questions

How to view client information?

To view client's information, simply hover over 'client' on the navigation tab and click on 'manage client'.

How to add a new client?

To add a new client, hover over 'client' on the navigation tab and click on 'add client'. Make sure to fill all the details before clicking submit.

How to search for a specific client?

To search for a specific client, hover over 'client' on the navigation tab and click on 'search client'. Fill the name of the company in the search bar and click search.

How to view document?

To view documents, simply hover over 'cdocument' on the navigation tab and click on 'manage document'.

How to add a new document?

To add a new document, hover over 'document' on the navigation tab and click on 'add document'. Make sure to fill all the details before clicking submit.

How to search for document from a specific company?

To search for document from a specific company, hover over 'docuemnt' on the navigation tab and click on 'search document'. Fill the name of the company in the search bar and click search.
